Our Story

A railway arch,
a single drum roaster.

We began with one obsession — what coffee tastes like when it's treated with patience. No investor deck, no shortcuts. Just a small room, good light, and beans we could stand behind.

Years on, we still roast everything ourselves, still know our growers by name, and still believe a morning is better when it's unhurried.
